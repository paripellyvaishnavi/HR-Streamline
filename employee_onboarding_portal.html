Employee Onboarding Portal

A digital onboarding experience that enhances new employee engagement and smoothens the onboarding process.

Project Overview

This onboarding portal is designed for new hires to complete paperwork, watch introductory videos, and become familiar with company policies and culture. It reduces manual HR work and provides a streamlined, user-friendly experience for employees.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vue.js)
  • Backend: Firebase

Key Features

  • Personalized dashboard for new employees with tasks and documents
  • Automated document submission with e-signatures
  • Integration with HRIS for automatic employee profile setup
  • Interactive tutorials and videos about company culture and policies

Challenges Faced

Ensuring seamless integration with existing HRIS and automating document submission while maintaining a user-friendly interface was a challenge. It required deep integration with APIs and maintaining security standards for document handling.

Solution and Outcome

The portal helped reduce onboarding time by 40% and improved new hire satisfaction through a smooth, informative, and engaging onboarding process. HR managers also reported a significant reduction in manual data entry errors.
